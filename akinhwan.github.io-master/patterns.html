ALLOVER PATTERNS

T hese works on pen and paper range from sizes of 8"x11" to 12'x18'. After an obsession and intrigue in contemporary decorative repeat patterns, I awoke from an intense dream and began with my first early original patterns. These drawings exhibit my perpetual need for variation and search for the infinite, within the simple duality of black and white, line and dot. They exhibit simple rules and obey an overall system, not perfectly symmetric, but regular and rhythmic. This is a selection of my favorite and most complete patterns. To purchase or license please get in touch.
